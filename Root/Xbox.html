Xbox starts off a new generation of consoles

On November 12th Xbox launched its next gen console, Xbox series x. I've had hands on with this console since day one and here are my thoughts.

The console has been a joy to use. Its whisper quite even after hours of use, it hasn't lagged once in any game and its constant output of Series x enchanced free upgrades ensures you never get bored. Backwards compatibility also enables you to keep your play area tidy due to you only needing this one console to play nearly every Xbox game ever made

Halo 4 gets its 4k 60fps upgrade

Halo the master chief collection has finally upgraded its final game in the original masterchief saga. Halo 4 has been upgraded for free to a full 4k 60fps experience on Xbox series x and pc. This has revamped the game for me. Although to not everyones taste halo 4 is one of my favourite games in the series. Its story is an evolution of the old formula and its gameplay is halo at its best. Even the xbox 360 version of this game looked absolutely wonderful so take it from me when i tell you this uograde is phenomenal and something everybody whos a fan of tha games needs to check out
