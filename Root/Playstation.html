Playstation starts off a new generation of consoles

Playstation launched its next gen console, Playstation 5. I've had hands on with this console since day one and here are my thoughts.

The console has been a joy to use. Its got a good line-up of games including astro's playroom and Miles moralis. Backwards compatibility also enables you to keep your play area tidy due to you only needing this one console to play two generations of Playstation games

Sony hits next gen...with alot of issues

Sony have launched their next gen piece of hardware, the ps5. This behemot of a console is selling fast, but was it rushed? Reports of console hardware level failure have been flooding the internet. People are getting scared as $500 pieces of hardware are starting to already show signs of wear and tear. Graphical issues, lag, screen tearing and artifacts on in game textures are just a few of the problems. Others include gpu failure and full console bricking when trying to use sleep mode or an external hard-drive. Sony is yet to address these issues but we'll report when they do
